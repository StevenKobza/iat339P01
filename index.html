Section CSS id: "branding"

Basic Branding Ideas

Escudo Power provides electrical services to many different companies based on their needs. As a basis, Escudo Power is focused primarily on renewable energy and providing solutions that allow for reduced long term impact on the planet.

The company is a solution provider to potential companies, they wish to find solid solutions that help the environment and reduce the amount of non-renewable energy we're using.

This is Escudo Power's logo, and as such I feel that the colours to use would be heavily based around orange and white as they are the colours on their logo, as well as using a slimmer sans serif font as it is the same style of Font that is used on their logo.

A font that I was also thinking about would be Raleway by Google Fonts https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Raleway
